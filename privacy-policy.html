Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 12, 2025  ·  Last updated: June 12, 2025

OurUsual is a simple order-tracking app. We built it to help families remember what they order — not to collect your data. This policy explains exactly what we do (and don't) do with your information.

Information We Collect

OurUsual does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. All data you enter into the app — including store names, locations, people's names, and order details — is stored locally on your device only.

We do not have access to your data. We do not receive it. It never leaves your device.

Data You Enter

The app stores the following types of information locally on your device:

  • Store or restaurant names and locations you add
  • Names of people in your household or group
  • Order details assigned to those people

This data is saved using your device's local storage and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

Analytics & Tracking

OurUsual does not use any analytics services, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking tools. We do not track how you use the app, how often you open it, or what you enter into it.

Third-Party Services

OurUsual may use Apple Maps to display or look up store locations. Apple's own privacy policy governs any data handled through that service. We do not share your data with Apple or any other third party.

Children's Privacy

OurUsual is designed for general family use. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Since all data stays on your device and is never transmitted to us, no special handling is required.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by removing entries within the app or by uninstalling the app entirely. Uninstalling OurUsual removes all locally stored data.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to check back periodically. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
