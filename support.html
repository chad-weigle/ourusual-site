Frequently Asked Questions

Does OurUsual store my data anywhere?

No. All data you enter — stores, orders, and names — is stored locally on your device only. Nothing is sent to or stored on any external server.

Does OurUsual sync to the cloud?

No. OurUsual does not connect to any cloud service. Your data lives on your device and stays there. If you get a new phone, you'll need to re-enter your information.

Can I share my orders with others?

No. OurUsual does not have any sharing or syncing features. The app is designed as a personal and family reference tool — your orders are private to your device.
